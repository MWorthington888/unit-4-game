Gem Dandy Collector Game

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four gems below. By clicking on a gem, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


The value of each gem is hidden until you click it.


You win by matching your total score to the random number.


You lose if your total score exceeds the random number.
